ABOUT ME

Hello! My name is Gyan. I like technology, design and cats.

I'm currently a UX Designer 2 at Microsoft, where I'm building AI powered tools for content creators. Before this, I was getting a master's degree in Interaction Design from IDC School of Design, studying fake news on WhatsApp, doing freelance graphic design, and making fonts. I use this website to document my work and some stray thoughts.
